Marvel, also known as Marvel Comics, is a well-known American entertainment company best known for its expansive superhero universe. Timely Publications, which was founded in 1939, introduced iconic characters such as Spider-Man, Iron Man, and the X-Men. Marvel's intricate storylines span comics, television shows, and blockbuster movies, delving into themes of heroism and identity. Its cultural influence extends beyond entertainment to fashion, technology, and social discourse.
